# Task 3 — Classical Probability

## Setup

Person $X$: time ∈ {4, 5, 6} hours, errors ∈ {0, 1, 2}. Total $|\Omega| = 9$ equally likely outcomes.

## Solutions

**1. Job completed in 4 hours:**

Favorable outcomes: $(4,0), (4,1), (4,2)$ → 3 outcomes.

$$\boxed{P = \frac{3}{9} = \frac{1}{3}}$$

**2. Flawlessly in 6 hours:** $(6, 0)$ → 1 outcome.

$$\boxed{P = \frac{1}{9}}$$

**3. At most 5 hours:**

Time ∈ {4, 5}: $(4,0),(4,1),(4,2),(5,0),(5,1),(5,2)$ → 6 outcomes.

$$\boxed{P = \frac{6}{9} = \frac{2}{3}}$$

**4. At most 5 hours AND at most 1 error:**

Time ∈ {4,5}, errors ∈ {0,1}: $(4,0),(4,1),(5,0),(5,1)$ → 4 outcomes.

$$\boxed{P = \frac{4}{9}}$$
