# Solution — Task 3: Permutations with Repeated Elements

## 1. Arrangements of MISSISSIPPI

Letters: M(1), I(4), S(4), P(2) → total 11 letters.

$$\frac{11!}{1! \cdot 4! \cdot 4! \cdot 2!} = \frac{39\,916\,800}{1 \cdot 24 \cdot 24 \cdot 2} = \frac{39\,916\,800}{1\,152} = 34\,650$$

## 2. Arrangements of STATISTICS

Letters: S(3), T(3), A(1), I(1), C(1) → total 10 letters.

$$\frac{10!}{3! \cdot 3! \cdot 1! \cdot 1! \cdot 1!} = \frac{3\,628\,800}{6 \cdot 6} = \frac{3\,628\,800}{36} = 100\,800$$

## 3. Arrangements of STATISTICS starting with S

Fix S in the first position. Remaining 9 letters: S(2), T(3), A(1), I(1), C(1).

$$\frac{9!}{2! \cdot 3! \cdot 1! \cdot 1! \cdot 1!} = \frac{362\,880}{2 \cdot 6} = \frac{362\,880}{12} = 30\,240$$
