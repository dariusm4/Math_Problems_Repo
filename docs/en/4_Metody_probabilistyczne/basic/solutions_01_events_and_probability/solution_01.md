# Solution

## Task 1 — Coin Tossing

1. Sample space for one coin toss:

\(\Omega_1 = \{H, T\}\).

2. Sample space for two coin tosses:

\(\Omega_2 = \{(H,H), (H,T), (T,H), (T,T)\}\).

3. Sample space for three coin tosses:

\(\Omega_3 = \{(H,H,H), (H,H,T), (H,T,H), (H,T,T), (T,H,H), (T,H,T), (T,T,H), (T,T,T)\}\).

4. Number of elementary outcomes:

- \(|\Omega_1| = 2\)
- \(|\Omega_2| = 4\)
- \(|\Omega_3| = 8\)

5. Interpretation of an elementary outcome:

- In \(\Omega_1\): the result of a single coin toss.
- In \(\Omega_2\): the ordered results of two tosses (first toss, second toss).
- In \(\Omega_3\): the ordered results of three tosses (first, second, third).
