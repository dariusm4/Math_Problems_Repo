# Solution

## Task 2 — Rolling a Die

1. Sample space for one roll of a fair six-sided die:

\(\Omega_1 = \{1,2,3,4,5,6\}\).

2. Sample space for two consecutive rolls:

\(\Omega_2 = \{(i,j) : i,j \in \{1,2,3,4,5,6\}\}\).

3. Sample space for three consecutive rolls:

\(\Omega_3 = \{(i,j,k) : i,j,k \in \{1,2,3,4,5,6\}\}\).

4. Number of elementary outcomes:

- \(|\Omega_1| = 6\)
- \(|\Omega_2| = 6 \times 6 = 36\)
- \(|\Omega_3| = 6 \times 6 \times 6 = 216\)

5. Interpretation of an elementary outcome:

- In \(\Omega_1\): one face of the die that appears in a single roll.
- In \(\Omega_2\): an ordered pair of faces from two consecutive rolls.
- In \(\Omega_3\): an ordered triple of faces from three consecutive rolls.
